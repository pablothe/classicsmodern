“Well for what, well why did I come to her now? I told her: it was a matter; what matter was it? There wasn’t any matter at all! To announce that I was coming; so what? What need was there! Do I love her, perhaps? But no, no, I’ve now driven her away like a dog. Does she really need me, Kreстов, in the end? Oh, how low I have fallen! No, – I needed to see her tears, I needed to see her fear, to watch her heart ache and torment itself! I had to grab hold of something, slow down, look at a person! And I dared to hope for myself like that, to dream of myself, a poor, insignificant man, a scoundrel, a scoundrel!”

He walked along the embankment of the canal, and it was not far for him. But having reached the bridge, he stopped and suddenly turned onto the bridge, in that direction, and went to Sennaya.

He greedily looked to the right and to the left, peered intently at every object, and could not focus his attention on anything; everything slipped away. “Here in a week, in a month they will take me somewhere in these convict carriages along this bridge, I will then look at this canal – it would be good to remember this? – flashed through his mind. – Here is this sign, I will then read these very letters? Here it is written: “Comrade,” well, let’s remember this a, letter a, and look at it a month later, at this very a: how will I then…”
