“Will you not leave me, Sonya?” he said, looking at her with almost hope.

“No, no; never and nowhere!” Sonya cried out, “I will follow you, everywhere I will follow!” “Oh, my Lord!.. Oh, I am unhappy!..” And why, why did I not know you before! Why did you not come before? “Oh, my Lord!”

“Here you have come.”

“Now! Oh, what shall we do now!.. Together, together!” she repeated as if in a trance, and again embraced him, “I will go to Siberia with you!” -

She pressed her lips.

— I, Sonya, don't even want to go to Siberia yet, — he said.

Sonya quickly looked at him.

After the first, passionate and agonizing sympathy for the unfortunate man, a terrible idea of murder suddenly struck her. In the changed tone of his words, she suddenly heard a murderer. She stared at him in amazement. She still knew nothing of why, how, or for what purpose this was. Now all these questions burst into her consciousness. And again she didn't believe: “He, he is a murderer! Is it even possible?”

— What is this! Where am I standing! — she exclaimed in deep bewilderment, as if not yet recovered, — how could you, you, such… have done this?.. What is this!

— Yes, of course, to rob. Stop, Sonya! — he answered wearily and even with annoyance.

Sonya stood as if stunned, but suddenly screamed:

— You were hungry! you… to help your mother? Yes?

— No, Sonya, no, — he muttered, turning away and hanging his head, — I wasn't so hungry… I really wanted to help my mother, but… and this is not entirely true… don't torment me, Sonya!

Sonya waved her hands.

— Surely, surely this is all true! Lord, what is the truth! Who can believe this?.. And how, how do you give everything last, and kill to rob? Ah! — she suddenly exclaimed, — those money that you gave to Katerina Ivanovna… those money… Lord, will they really…

— No, Sonya, — he interrupted hastily, — those money were not those,
calm down! Those money my mother sent to me, through a merchant, and I received them
sick, on the same day as I gave them… Razumikhin saw… he also received for
me… those my money, my own, real my.

Sonya listened to him in bewilderment and tried with all her might to understand
something.

— And those money… I, in truth, even do not know, were there money in them, -
he added quietly and as if in thought, — I took her purse from her neck, leather, full, tight purse… and I did not look into it; I didn’t have time, probably… Well and what things, some locks and chains – I buried all those things and the purse on a stranger’s yard, on the Vth avenue under a stone, on the next morning… Everything is there and now lies…

Sonya listened to him with all her might.

— Well, then, why… how you said: to rob, and you didn’t take anything
