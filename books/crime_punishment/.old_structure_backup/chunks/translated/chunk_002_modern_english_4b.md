be so openly speaking, am I not a fool at all? Let me reveal to you one
psychological peculiarity. Recently, justifying my love for Avdotya
Romanovna, I said that I myself was a victim. Well, know that I now
feel no love at all, n-no, so it is even strange to me, because I
actually felt something…

— From idleness and corruption, — interrupted Raskolnikov.

— Indeed, I am a corrupt and idle man. However, your sister has so many
advantages that I could not help but be impressed. But all this is nonsense,
as I now see.

— Have you seen this long ago?

— I began to notice it before, and finally I was convinced on the third
day, almost at the very moment of arrival in Petersburg. However, I
already imagined in Moscow that I was going to achieve the hand of
Avdotya Romanovna and compete with Mr. Luzhin.

— Excuse me, if you’ll excuse me, please do me a favor: can you shorten
it and go straight to the purpose of your visit? I am in a hurry, I must
go from the courtyard…
